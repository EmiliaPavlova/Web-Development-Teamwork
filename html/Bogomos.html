Bogomos

Distinguished in every way - intellect, appearance, bravery, skilover - yet he is tortured by a deep melancholy, the source of which follows.

Years ago while he was walking along a beach, he found a lamp. He rubed the lamp, and a genie appeared. (Ever heard about Alladin and the lamp? That's how it began...)

“I am the most powerful genie in the world. I can grant you any wish, but only one wish.” - the genie said.

Bogomos pulled out a map, pointed to it and said, “I’d want peace in the Middle East.”

The genie responded, “Gee, I don’t know. Those people have been fighting for millennia. I can do just about anything, but this is likely beyond my limits.”

Bogomos then said, “Well, I am a programmer, and my programs have lots of users. Please make all my users satisfied with my software and let them ask for sensible changes.”

At which point the genie responded, “Um, let me see that map again.”

Bogomos still dreams about his piece in the Middle East...
